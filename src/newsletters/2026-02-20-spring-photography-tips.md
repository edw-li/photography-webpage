## Welcome to Spring Shooting Season

Spring is arguably the most exciting time for photographers. With longer days, dramatic skies, and nature coming back to life, there's no shortage of subjects to capture.

### 1. Chase the Golden Hour

Spring golden hours are some of the most dramatic of the year. The sun sits lower on the horizon compared to summer, giving you a wider window of warm, directional light.

**Pro tip:** Arrive 30 minutes before sunset to scout your composition. The best light often comes in the final 10 minutes.

### 2. Embrace Overcast Days

Don't put your camera away when clouds roll in. Overcast skies act as a giant softbox, producing even, flattering light that's perfect for:

- Macro flower photography
- Portrait sessions
- Waterfall long exposures
- Forest canopy shots
