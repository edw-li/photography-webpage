## Happy New Year, Bridgeway!

2025 was our biggest year yet: we grew to over 50 members, hosted 24 workshops, and celebrated our first gallery exhibition. But 2026 is shaping up to be even better.

### New Equipment Library Additions

Thanks to our partnership with a local camera shop, we're adding **8 new lenses** and **2 lighting kits** to our equipment library this quarter. Members can borrow gear for up to one week at a time — no rental fees.

### Expanded Workshop Schedule

We're doubling down on education this year:

- **Monthly masterclasses** with guest photographers (first one in March!)
- **Beginner bootcamps** — a 4-week series starting in February
- **Post-processing labs** — hands-on editing sessions every other Thursday

### Gallery Partnerships

We're thrilled to announce partnerships with two new venues for member exhibitions:

1. **The Riverside Gallery** — quarterly rotating exhibitions starting April
2. **City Hall Atrium** — a permanent display wall for Bridgeway members

### Monthly Photo Challenges

New this year: **monthly themed photo challenges** with community voting and prizes. Each month will have a creative theme, and members submit their best single image. January's theme is *Urban Geometry* — start shooting!

### Membership Updates

- Annual dues remain at $50 for 2026
- Student discount: $25 with valid ID
- Family memberships now available: $75 for up to 3 members

Here's to another amazing year of photography and community. See you at the next meetup!
